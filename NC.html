Chakli

Chakli is a crispy and savory Indian snack made from rice flour and gram flour. The dough is seasoned with spices, shaped into spirals using a chakli maker, and deep-fried until golden brown. It's crunchy, flavorful, and perfect for festive occasions or tea-time munching!

Chivda

Chivda is a light and crunchy Indian snack mix made from flattened rice (poha), nuts, spices, and sometimes sev. It is typically seasoned with turmeric, salt, chili powder, and mustard seeds, creating a flavorful and savory treat. Perfect for munching any time!

Price: ₹152 130 gram

Aloo wadi

Aloo Wadi is a delicious Maharashtrian snack made from colocasia leaves (aloo) and a spiced gram flour (besan) paste. The leaves are coated with the paste, rolled, steamed, sliced, and then fried until crispy. It's a savory and flavorful treat, often enjoyed during festive occasions.

Kothimbir Wadi

Kothimbir Wadi is a flavorful Maharashtrian snack made from coriander leaves (kothimbir), gram flour (besan), and spices. The mixture is steamed, then cut into pieces and shallow-fried until crispy. Perfect as an appetizer or snack!
