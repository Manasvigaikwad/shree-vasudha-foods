Dink Ladoo

Dink Ladoo is an energy-boosting Indian sweet made from edible gum, nuts, and jaggery. It is especially beneficial in winter.

Besan Ladoo

Besan Ladoo is a popular Indian sweet made from gram flour (besan), ghee, and sugar. The flour is roasted in ghee until golden brown and aromatic, then mixed with sugar and shaped into round balls. These ladoos are rich, nutty, and melt-in-the-mouth delicious!

Price: ₹160 150 gram
