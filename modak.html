Ukdiche Modak

Modak is a sweet dumpling popular in Western and Southern India. The sweet filling on the inside of a modak consists of freshly grated coconut and jaggery while the outer soft shell is made from rice flour or wheat flour mixed with khava or maida flour. The dumpling can be fried or steamed. The steamed version (called ukdiche modak) is often eaten hot with ghee.
