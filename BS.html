Breakfast Special

Upma

Upma is a popular South Indian breakfast dish made from semolina (rava or sooji). It's cooked with vegetables, spices, and seasonings like mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and ginger. The result is a flavorful and savory porridge-like dish that's light yet satisfying!

Shira

Shira, also known as Sooji Halwa or Semolina Pudding, is a classic Indian dessert made from semolina (sooji or rava), ghee, sugar, and milk or water. It's cooked until thick and garnished with nuts and saffron. Rich, sweet, and comforting!

Price: ₹160 150 gram
